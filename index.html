Welcome

I'm Jay Billings, a designer and engineer for the web. I specialize in creating clean, maintainable, user-friendly web applications, but have the skills to tackle challenges from server to client.

I've been in the industry for 6 years and have worked with a wide range of organizations, from governmental agencies to international giants like Microsoft to local start-ups.
